Welcome to Space Weather


where we explore the dynamic and fascinating interactions between
the Sun and Earth's atmosphere. Space weather, driven by solar winds,
solar flares, and coronal mass ejections, can influence everything
from satellite communications to power grids. Stay informed on how these
cosmic events impact our planet and how scientists are working to predict
and mitigate their effects.
